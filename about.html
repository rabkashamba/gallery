About — Rabson Kashamba

Rabson Kashamba is a Development Economist and writer from Malawi. He is the author of the poetry collection "Whispers from Within," and the romance short story collection, "Fickle Fragile Flames." His writing reflects his deep connection to Malawi and explores a wide range of human experiences, from introspective reflections to the complexities of romantic relationships. Kashamba's unique blend of analytical thinking, informed by his work in development economics, and creative expression, makes his writing both insightful and engaging.

"I'm a development economist and writer, curious about how things work, with an inextinguishable drive to become a creator."

Rabson Kashamba
